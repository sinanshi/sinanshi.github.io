Building a platform for genetic inference from the Genomics England data

Genomics England is working on 100,000 human genome sequencings with high coverage data. We are responsible for edeveloping many of the leading current approaches for phasing, imputation, and inferring population struture and demography. I work on a mixture of algorithm development and implementation.
